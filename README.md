# 🛒 E-Commerce Performance & Customer Behavior Analysis

**Tools:** MySQL | Python (Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib) | Tableau | MS Word  
**Type:** End-to-End Data Analysis Project  
**Date:** March 2026

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes **10,000 orders** from a Bangladesh-based e-commerce business to uncover sales patterns, profit drivers, customer behavior, and regional performance gaps.

The full pipeline covers:
- Data extraction with **MySQL**
- Exploratory analysis & visualization with **Python**
- Interactive dashboard in **Tableau**
- Business report with actionable recommendations

---

## 📊 Key Findings

| Metric | Result |
|--------|--------|
| Total Sales | ৳2.54 Crore |
| Total Profit | ৳50.6 Lakh |
| Profit Margin | 19.90% |
| Total Orders | 10,000 |
| Avg. Discount | 12.38% |

- ✅ **Chittagong** leads in sales (৳52.66L) — Dhaka underperforms despite being the largest market
- ✅ **Accessories, Smartphone & Tablet** are the top 3 most profitable sub-categories
- ✅ **Sales peak** in Jan–Feb, drop sharply in June & September (seasonal pattern)
- ✅ **Discounts above 20%** show near-zero impact on sales or profit (correlation: -0.01)
- ✅ **Sales & Profit correlation: 0.86** — strong positive relationship

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. **Launch targeted marketing in Dhaka** — biggest market, lowest sales
2. **Stop high discounts (>20%)** — no measurable impact on revenue
3. **Run flash sales in June–September** to counter seasonal dip
4. **Scale Accessories & Smartphone inventory** — highest profit margin
5. **Introduce a Loyalty Program** instead of blanket discounts
